Solitaire

Solitaire by Jenetic comes with all the classic game play we know and love. Enjoy the fun, challenging, timeless game play of Klondike Solitaire, in the knowledge that our games and website never track you and we never sell, share, or store your personal data. With no adverts to interrupt your play, a single tap or drag interface, randomised daily challenges, multiple difficulty levels and rewarding achievements, Solitaire by Jenetic is like no other Solitaire game.
